Introduction

In a Nutshell

This multi day tour focuses at the Belum Temenggor Rainforest (an unspoilt rainforest paradise) over 130 million years old, covering 117,500 hectares of untouched paradise with 3,000 species of flowering plants, 3 species of the world’s largest flower – THE RAFFLESIA, 64 species of ferns 300 bird species 10 species of hornbills (Hornbill Conservation program by WWF), 14 most threatened mammals as in Elephants, Tigers, Rhino, Sun Bear, Tapirs, Deer etc.

Several organisations and the aborigines working with WWF and MNS are helping in the preservation and conservation of the rainforest and the endangered animals. Bird watching is a feature at the Belum.

FUSION FOOD Apart from the above, you will get a chance to taste various Malaysian cuisines.

Also you will get to swim in a Jungle Waterfalls tucked deep in the Malaysian Jungle.

And finally, we hope that during this 5-day tour, we hope you will have a fun and exciting experience.

Itinerary

Hotel : Belum Eco Resort or similar. Resort in the jungle. Need early booking ICONIC BATU CAVES blazing in rainbow colors. Climb 272 steps into the temple of Hindu Deity Lord Subramaniam who is honored during “Thaipusam” every year. Devotees give thanks for vows fulfilled. Ladies to be properly attired to enter the temple – knees and shoulders covered.

BELUM Eco RESORT The premier ecotourism holiday resort in the jungle where you get complete relaxation, stunning views, a chance to get closer to nature.

After Lunch continue on to Belum Rainforest. Check in to hotel. Free at leisure. Enjoy the rainforest ambience. Dinner at the resort.

NITE JUNGLE WALK Join Nature Guide in the “hunt” for lesser animals as in insects, spiders, scorpions, birds etc. You will enjoy the Forest Orchestra.

BOAT RIDE Catch a boat ride to starting point for a trekking experience. Walk through yet-to-bespoilt virgin jungle, trek to reach a rainforest waterfall. The experienced nature guide will belt out facts and taboos of the jungle, about the aborigines, the endangered animals. Medicinal and poisonous plants and the eager aphrodisiac plants. Ask the Guide.

JUNGLE TREKKING The trekking opens a world of many jungle surprises and experiences. The Nature guide will belt our facts and fancies of the Belum Rainforest.

ABORIGINE VILLAGE You will be greeted by the Aborigines and a fire making and blowpipe demonstration awaits you. They live carefree lives and the rainforest is their supermarket.

RAFFLESIA The World’s Largest flower is along the trekking route. (subject to bloom!).

HORNBILLS Look out for 10 species of hornbills that come crashing on tree tops..

RAINFOREST WATERFALLS Laze at this mesmerizing waterfalls deep in the Malaysian jungle. Swim and wallow as the mountain fresh waters caress your skin. Packed lunch will be served in a jungle ambience. Enjoy! Return to hotel for dinner.

Hotel: Bayview Resort, Batu Ferringghi or similar

Penang Island is now one of the prime tourist attractions in Malaysia and listed as a UNESCO Heritage site. Sir Francis Light founded Penang in 1786 and he first landed in Fort Cornwallis.

MALAYSIAN LUNCH at Georgetown, Penang.

CLAN JETTY The Chinese Communities of earlier years formed clans and lived on houses on stilts partly submerged in water and they had plank walkways.

TRISHAW RIDE The exhilarating ride should give one an insight of Georgetown – the heritage Capital of Penang Island.

BATU FERINGGHI Your 4 star beach hotel is located in the happening place of Penang Island – Batu Feringghi. It boasts of luxury resorts with well landscaped swimming pools, beautiful stretch of sugar white sandy beach, restaurants selling various cuisines.

You will be undisturbed to enjoy the sun, sea and sand. Your hotel boasts of a Spa, Jacuzzi, Gym, beach activities. The beach and the swimming pool beckons you. You are in for a blast! Dinner will be on your own. Enjoy!
