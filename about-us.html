About StarTravels

As one of the leading online travel companies, Startravel meets a wide range of travel needs on the same platform, helping customers to have great trips with relatives and friends.

With more than 100 domestic and international aviation partners, StarTravels brings more than 200,000 routes worldwide. Huge warehouse of travel tours with many genres and with many types of accommodation such as hotels, apartments, motels, homestays, villas and resorts.
