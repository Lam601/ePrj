Meals: No meals included

Hotel: Mercure Wangshang Beijing (4-Star)

After having breakfast in your hotel, you will meet your tour guide and other group members and we will take you directly to your first destination, Tiananmen Square by private car or van. Not only one of the largest squares in the world, this place is also famous for all the events that have taken place in Chinese history.

Cross the street is your next destination, The Forbidden City which is an imperial palace in Beijing that dates back to the Ming dynasty.

After lunch, we will have a stop at pearl factory and then head to Temple of Heaven, the largest ancient sacred place of worship in China with unique architecture located in the south east of Beijing. In the evening, you will go to a theater to watch exciting Chinese Kong Fu Show.

Meals: Western buffet breakfast, Lunch

After a full buffet breakfast at hotel, it is time to visit one of the 7 wonders of the world, The Great Wall of China. As you might know the Great wall is very long and there are several sections that are very famous to visitors and we will go to the Mutianyu section of Great Wall as it is less crowded than Badaling Great Wall and you can enjoy your time and take better pictures.

After lunch and a short stop at Jade Factory we will head to Summer Palace, a vast ensemble of lakes, gardens and palaces in Beijing.

Tea ceremony is one of the most interesting experiences in China. We wiill end our day visiting to a local tea house to drink Chinese tea live like locals and relax after an active day, then we will take you back to your hotel.

You start your 4th day by going to Summer Palace, a vast ensemble of lakes, gardens and palaces in Beijing which is a very famous tourist. After lunch, We will continue our trip to Hutong of Beijing and you can actually see the old neighborhoods of Beijing by walking around or sitting on a tricycle.

In the afternoon you will checkout and we transfer you to the train station to get on board high speed bullet train and continue your journey to Xian. Upon your arrival, we will drive you to the hotel in Xian.

Meals: Western buffet breakfast, Lunch

Hotel: Mercure Xian Renmin Square (4-Star)

The first day in Xian is dedicated to visit Terracotta Warriors and Houses Museum and you will admire the ancient craft of the Qin dynasty. There is a pottery-making factory that we will visit on our way to our lunch. We will go to a good local restaurant to try authentic taste of Chinese food based on your choice.

After lunch, you will visit Small Wild Goose Pagoda and Xian Museum and then transfer you to the hotel.

Meals: Western buffet breakfast, Lunch

The whole morning will be devoted to The City Wall where you can rent a bike and explore around on your own and take memorable pictures. After lunch, you will head to the airport by yourself since we do not include the transfer service for this part. You can book a taxi to the airport.

For any extended service after this one week China tour, you can consult our operators for some advice. If you need us to reserve the departure flight tickets, please feel free to contact us as well. Have a nice trip and welcome back!
